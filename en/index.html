What this call is for

When you feel stuck in a decision, the problem is rarely lack of intelligence. It is usually too much noise, fear and external pressure.

In this call I help you turn that noise down and listen to what you truly feel. Your truth is already there, it’s just not accessible with clarity right now.

By the end of the session you leave with clarity about what you want, inner safety to own your choice, and relief for finally knowing your next step.

What happens in the call

  • I listen with deep attention
  • I ask questions that take you to the essence
  • I capture what you say in between the lines
  • We look together at what feels true for you
  • I help you organise what feels messy inside so your decision becomes clearer

I do not tell you what to do and I do not decide for you. I hold space for your own truth to become visible.

Who this is for

  • you’ve been postponing a decision
  • you feel stuck in mental loops and “what ifs”
  • you’re afraid of making the wrong choice
  • you feel you already know the answer, but can’t trust it
  • you want to move forward with more ease and presence
  • the decision can be small or big, but it’s affecting your peace

Who this is not for

  • you want someone to tell you exactly what to do
  • you are not willing to be honest with yourself
  • you prefer to keep everything the same even though it no longer feels right

About me

I’m Mariana. For many years I made decisions from what was expected of me, not from what I truly felt. At some point the internal noise became so loud that I could no longer recognise myself in the life I was living.

That was the beginning of my way back to myself: therapy, spirituality, presence, intuition, more honest choices. Today I use that experience — and my natural sensitivity to what people really feel — to help others find clarity, trust their own truth and make decisions that actually respect who they are.

FAQ

How long is the call?

Around 30 minutes.

Does the decision need to be “big”?

No. The decision can be small or big. What matters is the impact it’s having on you internally.

Do I need to be clear about what I want before we start?

No. You can come feeling confused and not knowing how to explain. We will explore and organise it together.

What if I feel I need more support afterwards?

We can talk about ongoing guidance at the end of the call if it feels aligned for you.
